Leveling

The Warrior xp requirement is standerd so it is one of the easiest vocations to level up. For fast leveling I recommended going to Slime Hill ALONE and fighting metal slimes, metal medlies and liquid metal slimes. I said ALONE because if you have 3 other characters/players you have to split the xp into 1/4ths ,and if you are a lower level then your allies you gain even less xp! Trust me, it is eaiser to level on Slime Hill alone then with allies. But don't forget to put your money in the bank in case you die!

Equipment

In the Swords list at the very top is the Uber Falcon Blade and the Falcon Blade. The Uber Falcon Blade and Falcon Blade are only the best if your strength stat is higher then 135. For the recipes to all of the alchemy equipment for a Warrior Click Here. Some of the equipment can be bought. For swords you can buy the Falcon Blade at the new shop in Stormway once you've completed the campain. While the Fire Blade can be bought at Upover which is the final place you can buy from. For the shields the only one that can be bought is the Silver Shield, which can be found at the new shop in Stornway after you complete the campain. For the Helmets the only one you can buy is the Mythril Helm which can be found at Upover For the Chestplates the only one you can buy is the Gigant Armour which can be found at the new shop in Stornway once you've completed the campain. For the Legs you can buy the Sturdy Slacks at the Secret Shop in Dourbridge while the Warrior's Trousers can be found at Bloomingdale around halfway through the campain where you get the boat. For the Shoes you can buy the Warrior's Boots at Bloomingdale around halfway through the campain where you get the boat.

Combining Other Vocations

This section is about taking other vocations and using there abilities and base stat upgrades to make the warrior stronger and have a wider range of abilities. All vocations have different weapon skills and some even have the shield skill. As you level you gain skill points which you put into these skill areas to increase your attack, defence, or gain abilities that only apply if you have that weapon group or a shield equipped. But each vocation has their own special skill group and the abilities and stats you gain from that skill group you keep even when you change vocations. We can use this to gain strength, defence, and more abilities from multiple vocations. Each skill area takes 100 skill points to complete which you can gain 100 skill points by leveling to level 38. To start go to Alltrades Abby and change to a Warrior if you havn't already. Then level up to 38 at Slime Hill and put all the skill points into the Warrior's Courage skill. Then go change to a gladiator at Alltrades Abby and level to 38 again. And put all the skill points into the Guts skill for a gladiator. Then do the exact same thing for a Paladin And put all 100 skill points into the paladin's Virtue skill. Then do the same with a martial artist and put all 100 into the martial artist's Focus skill. And now your Ultimate Warrior is complete!
