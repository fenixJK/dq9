Priest's Alchemy


This section will have all the recipes to the equipment for the Priest section. If you don't know if it requires alchemy go back to the previous section and next to each peice of equipment there is a symbol. There are three symbols, the * means it is made with alchemy, the # means it can be bought, and the ^ means it is a boss or mob drop. There is a list for each peice of equipment for the priest. The lists will only show equipment that requires alchemy.
